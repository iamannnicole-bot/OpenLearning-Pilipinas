OpenLearning Pilipinas 2026 Event Hub

A dedicated event website created for OpenLearning Pilipinas 2026, bringing together educators, industry leaders, government partners, and innovators to explore the future of learning, AI, and employability.

Features
📅 Event information and agenda
📝 Attendee registration and inquiry forms
🤝 Networking and connection requests
📸 Photo upload and event gallery submissions
🎤 Speaker and partner information
🎁 Access to event resources and materials
📬 Lead capture and post-event follow-up
🔗 Future collaboration and partnership opportunities
Purpose

The platform acts as a digital extension of the event, allowing attendees to:

Access event details before and during the event
Share photos and memorable moments
Express interest in OpenLearning solutions
Connect with other participants and institutions
Receive post-event resources, updates, and future opportunities

Built to support a seamless attendee experience while helping the OpenLearning Philippines team strengthen engagement, community building, and long-term institutional partnerships.
